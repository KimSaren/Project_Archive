Bing Bong Bingo

General information

To fill your bingo sheet, simply choose a cell and start typing on the searchbar. Selecting an option presented in the list will fill the cell with the corresponding selection.

The currently selected tile will be highligted as red. Clicking the tile again will undo your selection. Please note that you cannot change the "free space" in the middle.

Use the buttons below to help you format your table and start the game when you feel ready.

To play the game, simply click on a cell to mark it as crossed. Click it again to uncross it. Each consistent horizontal, vertical or diagonal line of crosses will count as a bingo. The free space in the middle can always be crossed.

You can use the button below to return back to filling the bingo sheet. Note, however, that this will reset your current progress.

Have fun!
